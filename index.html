FAQ

How is DeCenter different from traditional cloud platforms?

DeCenter combines real-world data centers operated by IPTP with community-powered compute nodes (e.g., PCs, phones, VPS). This hybrid architecture ensures low latency, transparency, and global scalability – beyond what centralized providers offer.

Is it safe to share my device with DeCenter?

Yes. Guardian Nodes manage key distribution, monitor behavior, and enforce security standards. You maintain full control over your device and can opt in/out anytime.

How is RWA Vault different from DeFi?

Unlike traditional DeFi protocols based on synthetic yields, the RWA Vault is backed by physical assets – like real data centers – and actual service revenue from AI builders and compute users.

Why do AI models need ethical auditing?

As AI expands into sensitive areas like education, healthcare, and media, unchecked outputs can be biased, offensive, or even dangerous. DeCCM ensures AI agents meet ethical standards before deployment.

What is the ELO Score and how does it affect me?

ELO is your personal reputation score as an auditor. Accurate, consistent reviews raise your ELO, unlocking harder tasks and better rewards.
